The People's Referendum

The People Demand Divestment

Although calls for ceasefire and divestment grow both inside and outside of the University, the University continues to ignore our calls for divestment and furthermore criminalize and repress pro-Palestinian protestors.

TAHRIR Coalition is launching a grassroots campaign for divestment and a more democratic university, called Divest / Don't Arrest.

The first stage of this campaign is a People's Referendum. This referendum is a response to the University's unilateral and undemocratic cancellation of the CSG vote last November and refusal to act on calls for divestment from representative bodies in the University, including CSG (which voted to divest from Israel in 2017) and the Faculty Senate (which voted to divest from Israel this January). It is also a response to the University's criminal repression of over 40 pro-Palestine student protestors.

The referendum will be open to all students, staff, and faculty on all three U-M campuses. This is an opportunity for the people to assert that we want divestment, and we want it now.
